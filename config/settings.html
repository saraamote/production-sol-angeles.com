    E - Social Feeds
 
The default client id is only for testing purposes. We highly recomend generating your own client id when you're store is live. Each client id has a limit request per hour so by creating your own you'll have a more reliable instagram feed.
Step 1 - Go here and then click the Register Your Application button. Fill in the form and complete the authorisation
Step 2 - Click here to create a new Client ID. Use any name, and description. Use your store url for the website and OAuth redirect_uri. Make sure to untick the two boxes 'Disable implicit OAuth' & 'Enforce signed header'.
Step 3 - Copy and paste your Client Id into the box below.
